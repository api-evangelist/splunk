Splunk is launching purpose-built AI agents to help security teams accelerate threat detection, investigation, and response across the SOC. The company introduced Automated Threat Analysis, Entity Analytics, and deeper AWS integration, positioning these tools as agentic-led and human-governed systems that amplify analyst expertise. These advancements address the challenge of defending against increasingly automated, machine-speed attacks while reducing manual investigative work.
