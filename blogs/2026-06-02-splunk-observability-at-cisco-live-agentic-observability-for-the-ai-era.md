Splunk announced new AI-driven observability innovations at Cisco Live 2026, designed to help operations teams detect and resolve incidents faster. The company introduced Agentic Observability, combining AI-powered tools like AI SRE agents with expanded monitoring for AI infrastructure and unified signal analysis. These advances aim to move teams from reactive incident response to proactive resolution, stating the goal as moving from "something is broken" to "here's what happened, why it matters, and what to do next."
